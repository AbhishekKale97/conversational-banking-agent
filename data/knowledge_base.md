# Intelligent Bank Knowledge Base

## Savings Accounts
- Savings accounts may require minimum monthly average balance depending on account variant.
- KYC is mandatory for account opening and periodic re-KYC may be required.

## Loans and Credit
- EMI usually follows reducing balance methodology unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- Lenders assess repayment capacity using income, obligations, credit history, and score.
- Penal charges and late payment fees vary by product and institution.

## Payments and Transfers
- IMPS is typically instant and available 24x7.
- NEFT is available in scheduled settlement batches.
- RTGS is used for high-value transfers and is generally near real-time during banking windows.

## Cards and Security
- Card tokenization replaces card details with secure tokens for online transactions.
- Customers should never share OTP, CVV, or PIN with anyone.

## Compliance and Customer Support
- Financial advice should be contextual and not treated as guaranteed outcomes.
- Final charges and eligibility are always governed by official lender policy and sanction terms.
